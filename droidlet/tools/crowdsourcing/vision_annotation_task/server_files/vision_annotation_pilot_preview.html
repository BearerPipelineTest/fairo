Mark or unmark blocks or air to complete each task

Each question will prompt you to mark or unmark a subset of the blocks pictured.


You will see two windows below each question.
  • The window on the left shows the Original Scene before any annotation has taken place.
  • The window on the right is the Annotation Window that you will use to complete the task.


Marked blocks are shown in translucent green. Unmarked blocks are any other color. Air has no color. Your cursor will turn the block you are about to mark/unmark translucent red.


To begin, click your mouse in the Annotation Window. You can use the arrow keys to rotate the view. Both the right and left windows will move at the same time.


You can perform three types of actions, examples of which are shown below:
  • Hold down 'a' and click on an unmarked block to mark it
  • Hold down 's' and click on an air block to mark it
  • Hold down 'd' and click on a marked block to change it back to what it was before

You may also press z at any time to undo your last action


When you are finished annotating, you must click the green 'Finish Annotation' button below each pair of windows to record your answer. If you do not see the shape pictured, instead click the red 'The Object is Not Present' button.


When you are finished, please answer the question at the bottom and click 'Submit'
